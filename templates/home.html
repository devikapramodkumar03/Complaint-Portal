How It Works

1

Register & File

Sign up and submit your complaint with details and a photo.

2

Verification

Our system verifies and assigns your complaint to the right department.

3

Official Action

The assigned official begins work to address and resolve the issue.

4

Real-time Tracking

Monitor the progress of your complaint live on your dashboard.

5

Resolution

Get notified as soon as your complaint is resolved and closed.
